From the plot, low use individuals typically wear their devices for the entire day or at least half the day. Though their steps are rather low, which could implicate that they are using the device as a watch, rather than to track steps. Moderate Users typically have higher average steps for both all day users and most day users. High use, users are similar to the moderate use users, however they have a large portion of users who used the device for less than half a day, and had relatively reduced steps as well. Although this could just be due to variability as a result of the limited sample size.

Overall, there isn’t really a clear pattern between total steps and device usage.
